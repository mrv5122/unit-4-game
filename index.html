Crystal Collector

Here are the rules to play:


  1. You'll be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • White
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Yellow
  3. Clicking on a gem will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each gem.
