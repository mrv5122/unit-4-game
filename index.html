Crystal Collector


How To Play


You'll be given a random number at the start of the game, this is the "Number To Get".


There are 4 gems below. Each gem has a set value.


Clicking on a gem will add its value to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the Number to Get.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the Number to Get.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game restarts, the gems will be assigned new values.
